Mary has cherished animals ever since she was a child in Vermont growing up with her loving dog, Slick, and sweet cat, Seymour. Over the years, Mary has adopted all of her animals and currently shares a dog, Emmett, and cat, Baby with her husband and two daughters. There are so many animals that need a home; please consider adoption first!

Lukee Forbes is a New York City native bringing compassion for animals and business development expertise to Rescue U and the Capital Region. Kat Kennedy grew up riding horses with the Saratoga County 4H Equestrian Club near her home in Charlton, NY. After becoming an Equine Specialist in 2001, she continued her education of therapeutic horsemanship as a Therapeutic Riding Instructor. “I love sharing my knowledge of horsemanship with others and I believe horses are therapeutic for so many reasons. I feel very grateful to be a part of the Rescue U team.”
