OUR MISSION

Visitation therapy~ lifting spirits one animal at a time.

RESCUE U is a not-for-profit organization that beleives that during times of uncertainty each person should be afforded access to therapeutic care to relieve stress and evoke feelings of peace and tranquility. While human interaction is important, therapy can come in many different forms and we let animals provide an additional sense of comfort in all types of difficult situaitons.

OUR STAFF

Kat Kennedy

Kat Kennedy grew up riding horses with the Saratoga County 4H Equestrian Club near her home in Charlton, NY. Kat showed locally and participated in the high school equestrian team. After becoming an Equine Specialist in 2001, she continued her education of therapeutic horsemanship with PATH Intl. as a Therapeutic Riding Instructor. “I love sharing my knowledge of horsemanship with others and I believe horses are therapeutic for so many reasons. I feel very grateful to be a part of the Rescue U team.”
