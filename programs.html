Equine Therapy

Rescue U offers customized therapeutic riding services to individuals from youth to adults. Programs are developed based upon the individual needs of the student. We work on a range of goals to assist in addressing specific areas including but not limited to muscular, cognitive, speech/communication, listening skills, anxiety/stress reduction, and other objectives. Therapy sessions take place at Badger Honey Farms in Schaghticoke, New York. For more information on our equine therapy program please inquire on the contact page.
