Programs and Services

Rescue A Pet

Give a new home to an amimal in need.

Adopt your newest family member today. We are an all breed rescue dedicated to finding good dogs good homes - regardless of breed. We place our dogs into temporary foster homes where they are socialized, spayed/neutered, vaccinated, and treated for any medical or behavioral conditions which would otherwise limit their adoptability.

A Veteran's Best Friend

Therapy animal visits

Therapy animals can provide relief for victims of PTSD, anxiety and depression by providing sweet furry love, which creates a calming and bonding effect. Our therapy animals are available for visits to veteran hospitals, assisted living Homes, senior centers, etc. Please contact us to inquire about arranging a visit for one of our sweet furbabies just waiting to bring love and unconditional acceptance to those who need it most.

Equine Therapy

Rescue U offers customized therapeutic riding services to individuals from youth to adults. Programs are developed based upon the individual needs of the student. We work on a range of goals to assist in addressing specific areas including but not limited to muscular, cognitive, speech/communication, listening skills, anxiety/stress reduction, and other objectives. Therapy sessions take place at Badger Honey Farms in Schaghticoke, New York. For more information on our equine therapy program please inquire on the contact page.

Your generous contribution to RESCUE U goes directly to funding the programs and services that benefit individuals right here in our community.
